Background

St. Joseph of Nazareth High School is a gem of an institution located in the heart of Mpigi, Uganda. The school boasts of ultra-modern facilities that provide a conducive learning environment for students. From the moment you step into the school, you are greeted by competent teaching and non-teaching staff who are dedicated to ensuring that students receive quality education. The school’s curriculum is rich and is blended with Godly values and extra-curricular activities. This approach to education helps to groom all-round individuals who are capable, analytical, constructive, and independent thinkers. The commitment to providing quality education extends beyond the classroom. St. Joseph of Nazareth High School encourages engagement in co-curricular activities, which helps to develop students’ talents and interests. The core values of St. Joseph of Nazareth High School are integrity, commitment, faithfulness, and excellence. These values guide the journey of every student who passes through the school. It’s no wonder that the school was ranked number one in Mpigi. The first-class facilities and a committed staff create an environment that is conducive to success. The school’s vision is to be the leading mixed boarding secondary school in Uganda, producing capable, analytical, constructive, and independent thinking citizens. At St. Joseph of Nazareth High School, the future of Uganda is being shaped. The school aims to provide quality education in a caring environment that will develop God-fearing, self-worthy, academically sound, and responsible citizens and future leaders. At St. Joseph of Nazareth High School, students discover the treasures of education and are equipped with the skills and values to make a difference in the world.
